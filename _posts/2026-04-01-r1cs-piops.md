## R1CS univariate PIOPs

 - **Sonic**[^MBKM19] (2019): First universal and updatable SRS for R1CS-like constraints, using Laurent polynomials and a bivariate encoding of the constraint system.
 - **Aurora**[^BCRplus19e] (2019): Transparent IOP for R1CS based on a novel univariate sumcheck over multiplicative subgroups, compiled via RS proximity testing.
 - **AuroraLight**[^Gabi19] (2019): Hybrid of Aurora and Sonic ideas; adapts Aurora's algebraic trick (connecting a polynomial's constant coefficient to its sum on a subgroup) into a Sonic-like SRS setting, replacing Laurent polynomials.
 - **Marlin**[^CHMplus19e] (2019): Introduces the algebraic holographic proof (AHP) abstraction for R1CS, achieving preprocessing via holography with a universal and updatable SRS.
 - **Fractal**[^COS19] (2019): Holographic IOP for R1CS, designed for recursive proof composition with transparent (no trusted) setup. Concurrent with Marlin but a distinct holographic protocol.

## R1CS multilinear PIOPs

 - **Spartan**[^Sett19e] (2019): Foundational sumcheck-based PIOP for R1CS over multilinear extensions. Reduces R1CS satisfiability to multilinear polynomial evaluations via the sumcheck protocol.
 - **Gemini**[^BCHO22e] (2022): Elastic PIOP for R1CS that "splits" multilinear polynomials into multiple univariate polynomials, enabling a tradeoff between linear-time/linear-space and quasilinear-time/log-space proving.

{: .warning}
Many well-known SNARKs for R1CS (e.g., Brakedown[^GLSplus21e], Orion[^XZS22e], Testudo[^CGGeplus23ee]) reuse **Spartan's PIOP** and contribute a new polynomial commitment scheme rather than a new PIOP.

## PLONKish univariate PIOPs

 - **PLONK**[^GWC19] (2019): Novel PIOP for PLONKish constraint systems (gate equations + copy constraints via a permutation argument over a multiplicative subgroup), with a universal and updatable SRS.

## PLONKish multilinear PIOPs

 - **HyperPlonk**[^CBBZ22e] (2022): Adapts PLONK-style arithmetization to the boolean hypercube using multilinear polynomials, with new sub-protocols for permutation checks and zero-checks via the sumcheck protocol.
 - **SuperSpartan**[^STW23eCCS] (2023): PIOP for CCS (Customizable Constraint Systems), which generalizes both R1CS and PLONKish. Extends Spartan's sumcheck approach to handle CCS natively.
